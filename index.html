Spy Family

A Japanese anime series about a spy who creates a fake family to complete his mission.

Loid Forger

A master spy who goes undercover as Loid Forger, a psychiatrist.

Yor Forger

An ordinary office clerk at Berlint City Hall, she also leads a secret life as a Garden assassin under the code name "Thorn Princess".

Anya Forger

Adopted daughter of Loid and Yor Forger. She attends Eden Academy as a first-year student in Cecile Hall, Class 3.

Overview

Spy × Family is an anime series about a master spy who creates a fake family to infiltrate a political leader's inner circle. The series combines comedy, action, and family drama.

Plot

Loid creates a new family with Yor Briar, a fake wife, and Anya Forger, an adopted daughter. Loid's family has secrets of their own: Yor is an assassin, and Anya has telepathic abilities.

Animated

The first season of the anime series Spy × Family began airing on April 9, 2022. The season was split into two parts, or cours, with the first part airing from April to June and the second part airing from October to December.
